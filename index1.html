Paneer is a fresh, non-aged cheese that is made by curdling hot milk with a food acid like lemon juice or vinegar. The curdled and coagulated milk particles are collected in a muslin to drain the extra whey. Either the muslin bundle is kept under a heavy weighted object or it is hung over a hook or the kitchen sink faucet handle for the whey to be drained. for more info and recipes INFO

Ingredients

2 tablespoons Butter or 1 tablespoon oil + 1 or 2 tablespoons butter

1 tej patta (Indian bay leaf), optional
